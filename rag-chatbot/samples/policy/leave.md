# Leave Policy

## Annual leave
Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid annual leave per year, accrued monthly. Up to 5 unused days may be carried into the next year; anything above 5 is forfeited on 31 December.

## Sick leave
Notify your manager before 10:00 on the first day of absence. A doctor's note is required for absences longer than 3 consecutive working days.

## Requesting leave
Submit leave requests at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal. Requests are approved by your direct manager.
