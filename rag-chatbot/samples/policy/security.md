# Security Policy

## Passwords and 2FA
Passwords must be at least 12 characters and unique to company systems. Two-factor authentication is mandatory for all email and admin accounts.

## Devices
Company laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled. Report a lost or stolen device to IT within 24 hours so it can be remotely wiped.

## Handling customer data
Customer data may only be stored in approved systems. Never email customer data to personal accounts or copy it to unencrypted USB drives.
