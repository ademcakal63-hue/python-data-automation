# Expense Policy

## Reimbursable expenses
Travel, client meals, and approved software subscriptions are reimbursable. Submit receipts within 30 days of the expense through the HR portal. Expenses over $200 require prior manager approval.

## Travel
Economy class is standard for flights under 6 hours. Hotel stays should not exceed $180 per night without prior approval. Use the corporate booking tool where possible.

## Non-reimbursable
Personal entertainment, fines, and in-room minibar charges are not reimbursable.
