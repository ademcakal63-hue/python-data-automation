# Shipping & Delivery

## Delivery times
Standard shipping is 3-5 business days. Express shipping is 1-2 business days. International orders take 7-14 business days plus customs clearance.

## Tracking an order
A tracking number is emailed when the order ships. You can also find it under Orders > [your order] > Track.

## Lost or delayed packages
If tracking has not updated in 5 business days, contact support and we will open a carrier investigation. A replacement ships once the carrier confirms the loss.
