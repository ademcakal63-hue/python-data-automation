# Billing & Payments

## Double or duplicate charges
If you were charged twice, it is almost always a temporary authorization hold that drops off in 3-5 business days. If a real duplicate charge has settled, contact support with both transaction IDs and we will refund the duplicate within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

## Refunds
Refunds go to the original payment method and take 5-10 business days to appear. Annual plans are refundable on a pro-rata basis within the first 30 days.

## Updating a payment method
Go to Settings > Billing > Payment method to add or change your card. The new card is charged on your next billing date.

## Failed payments
If a payment fails we retry for 7 days. After the grace period the account is paused, not deleted.
