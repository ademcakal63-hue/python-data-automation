# Account & Access

## Reset your password
Use the "Forgot password" link on the login page. The reset link is valid for 1 hour.

## Two-factor authentication (2FA)
Enable 2FA under Settings > Security. We support authenticator apps (TOTP). SMS 2FA is being deprecated.

## Close or delete an account
Account deletion is permanent and removes all data after a 30-day grace period. Email support from the account's registered address to start deletion.
