# Plans & Pricing

## Plans
There are three plans: Free, Pro, and Business. Free includes 3 projects and 1 integration. Pro is billed per seat monthly and unlocks unlimited projects. Business adds SSO, audit logs, and full-workspace exports.

## Seats and billing
You are billed per active seat. Adding a seat mid-cycle is prorated; removing a seat credits the unused time to your next invoice.

## Trials
Pro and Business offer a 14-day free trial with no card required. At the end of the trial the workspace drops to Free unless a plan is selected.
