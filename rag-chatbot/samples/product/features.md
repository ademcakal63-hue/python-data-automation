# Product Features

## Workspaces and projects
Each account has one workspace. Inside a workspace you create projects, and inside a project you create boards. Free plans are limited to 3 projects; paid plans are unlimited.

## Integrations
We integrate with Slack, Google Drive, and webhooks. Connect them under Settings > Integrations. Each integration can be scoped to a single project or the whole workspace.

## Exporting data
You can export any board to CSV or JSON from the board menu (... > Export). Full-workspace exports are available on the Business plan and are delivered as a downloadable archive by email.
